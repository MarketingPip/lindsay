Our History

Established in 2008

Passion, experience and a fierce commitment to education are the cornerstones of our success in the beauty industry.

I will make sure it is...After graduating from Elegance Schools Inc in 2018 I found my passion for waxing and body/facial treatments. After four years of experience in full body waxing, I am excited to bring my services to the Grand Bend and surrounding area to get us all 'Beach Ready'. The Beachy Peach is for the regular Beach Bum who wants to have a personalized hair-free experience with a mix of hard and soft waxes to ensure the least amount of pain, in the most quick and efficient manner. After all, we all want to have more time for our toes in the water, peach in the sand. See you soon, Lindsay xo
